About Us

Who We Are

We are a dedicated community of Nissan GT-R R34 enthusiasts, united by a shared passion for this monumental piece of JDM history. Our mission is to serve as the ultimate hub for information, history, and stunning photography of the machine affectionately known as "Godzilla." We strive to keep its legend thriving for generations of car lovers.

Driven by passion, fueled by the RB26DETT.
